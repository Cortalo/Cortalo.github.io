### Example 01

$$
\begin{aligned}
\int_{1}^{2}xdx &= 4\int_{1}^{2}\frac{1}{2}x \cdot \frac{1}{2} dx \\
&= 4 \int_{1/2}^{1}t dt
\end{aligned}
$$

### Example 02

$$
\begin{aligned}
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\frac{dy}{a^2+y^2} &= -j\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\frac{jdy}{a^2-(jy)^2} \\
&= -j\int_{C}\frac{dz}{a^2-z^2}
\end{aligned}
$$
